## Overview

Veripharm needed a consistent, accessible UI layer across a suite of compliance tools used by regulated pharmaceutical companies.

## Problem

Five product teams were building in isolation. Components diverged, accessibility standards were inconsistently applied, and new feature development was slowed by constant design rework.

## Approach

Audited the full product surface, identified ~200 unique component patterns, and consolidated them into a token-based system with clear usage guidelines. Built in WCAG 2.1 AA compliance from the ground up.

## Outcome

Time spent on design QA dropped by 60%. The system was adopted by all five product teams within two quarters.
