## Overview

Alpaca needed a consumer-facing trading experience that matched the trust and reliability expected in fintech, while staying approachable for new investors.

## Problem

The existing interface assumed heavy financial literacy and created friction at every step — from account setup to executing a first trade.

## Approach

We ran discovery sessions with both novice and experienced traders to map where confidence broke down. From that, I built a design system grounded in clarity: progressive disclosure of complexity, inline education, and a visual hierarchy that surfaced the right information at the right moment.

## Outcome

Reduced time-to-first-trade by 40%. Onboarding completion improved significantly after simplifying the KYC flow into a guided, step-by-step process.
