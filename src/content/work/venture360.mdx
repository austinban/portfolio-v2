## Overview

Venture360 serves fund managers who need to wrangle complex cap tables, investor reporting, and portfolio tracking in one place.

## Problem

Power users had outgrown spreadsheets but found the existing product too rigid. Key workflows — like preparing quarterly LP updates — took hours and involved manual data assembly.

## Approach

I mapped the full fund lifecycle and identified the three highest-friction moments: data entry, report generation, and LP communication. We redesigned each as a guided flow with smart defaults and auto-populated templates.

## Outcome

Report generation time dropped from ~3 hours to under 20 minutes. NPS improved 18 points in the quarter following the redesign.
